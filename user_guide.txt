
Main Page:
Can create new surveys by inputting a survey name.
Also lists all surveys in the system.

Survey Page:
If you are the user who created the survey, 
You have the option to delete questions, add questions, or delete the survey.

All users also have a list to all questions.
You can then choose any question to answer or change your answer to.

Questions Page:
You choose your answer to the question.

Results page:
Lists all results to the survey.
